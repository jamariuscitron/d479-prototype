Arriving to Taniti

Air Travel

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air. The island is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is currently expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Cruise Ship Arrival

Some visitors arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week.

Ground Transportation

Public & Private Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis & Rental Cars

Taxis are available in Taniti City. Rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport.

Bikes & Walking

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors, and helmets are required by law. Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay near Merriton Landing, which is easy to explore on foot.
